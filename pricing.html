Common questions

Can I switch plans later?

Yes — upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time. When you upgrade mid-cycle you're prorated for the remainder of the billing period.

What happens after the 14-day trial?

After the trial you can pick any paid plan or continue on the free Starter tier — no credit card needed to start, and we'll always remind you before charging anything.

How does per-request pricing work for overage?

On the Pro plan there's no hard cap — you pay a flat monthly fee for unlimited requests. On Starter, requests above 100/day are queued until the next day or you can upgrade.

Do you offer nonprofit or student discounts?

Yes! Registered nonprofits and verified students get 50% off Pro, billed annually. Email hello@neuralflow.ai with proof of eligibility.
