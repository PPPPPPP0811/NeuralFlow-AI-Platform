// faq

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before you get started.

What AI models does NeuralFlow support?

NeuralFlow currently supports 8+ models including GPT-4o, Claude 3.7 Sonnet, Gemini 1.5 Pro, Mistral, and more. We add new models regularly — and smart routing picks the best one for each request automatically.

Do I need a credit card to start?

No. Your 14-day Pro trial starts the moment you sign up — no credit card required. At the end of the trial you can choose a plan or continue on the free Starter tier.

How does smart model routing work?

Our routing layer classifies each incoming request by task type (summarization, code generation, Q&A, etc.) and selects the most cost-effective model that meets the quality bar. Customers save an average of 60% on inference costs compared to always using a flagship model.

Is my data used to train your models?

Never. Your prompts and completions are never used to train any model. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest, and you can opt into zero-data-retention mode for the most sensitive workloads.

What does the 99.9% uptime SLA mean in practice?

It means we guarantee NeuralFlow is available at least 99.9% of every calendar month. If we fall short, you receive service credits automatically — no need to file a claim. Enterprise plans include a 99.99% SLA with dedicated failover infrastructure.

Can I self-host or deploy in my own VPC?

Yes — private VPC deployment is available on Enterprise plans. You get all the NeuralFlow features running inside your own cloud environment, fully air-gapped from external traffic if needed.
